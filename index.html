Clone Hero is a free and open-source rhythm game developed by Srylain and a community of dedicated developers. It was initially released in March 2017 as a clone of the popular Guitar Hero game series, hence the name "Clone Hero." The game has gained a large following since its release, thanks to its user-friendly interface, extensive customization options, and a massive library of songs.

In Clone Hero, players use a guitar controller or keyboard to play along with the notes displayed on the screen, similar to Guitar Hero. The game supports a wide variety of guitar controllers, including those from the Guitar Hero and Rock Band series, as well as custom-built guitars. The game also includes support for drum and bass controllers, as well as support for multiple players.
One of the significant advantages of Clone Hero is its extensive song library, which is constantly growing thanks to the community of dedicated modders and developers. The game includes a vast array of user-created content, including custom songs, charts, and themes. Players can download and install these user-created mods directly from the Clone Hero website or through third-party sources, making it easy to customize the game to their liking.
